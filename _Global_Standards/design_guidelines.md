# Pearl Abyss - Global Design & Copy Guidelines
*These rules must be followed by Sarah and Lily across all projects.*

## 1. Aesthetic Identity (Sarah)
- **Vibe:** Elite, premium, modern, and highly polished.
- **Colors:** Deep dark modes by default, accented with vibrant, glowing colors (e.g., Purple/Blue gradients).
- **UI Elements:** Heavy use of glassmorphism (frosted glass), subtle micro-animations on hover, and clean rounded borders.
- **Typography:** Modern Sans-Serif fonts (e.g., Inter, Outfit, or Roboto).

## 2. Copywriting Tone (Lily)
- **Voice:** Professional, confident, concise, and highly persuasive.
- **Formatting:** Use short paragraphs, bold text for emphasis, and bullet points for scannability.
- **Documentation:** README files must be pristine, with clear step-by-step instructions and emojis for visual separation.
